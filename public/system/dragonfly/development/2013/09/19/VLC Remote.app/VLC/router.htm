The remote address lets VLC Remote know where to contact your computer that is running VLC.

If you know how your network is setup, then you can use your computer name on the network. This might be something like 'RobMac.HomeRouter' or just 'RobMac'.

If you don't know how your network is set up, then simply open http:www.HobbyistSoftware.com/ip.php on your computer (not your iPhone) and it should show you what your local IP address is. This will be something like '192.168.1.10'.

You can then enter the ip address into the 'Remote Address' field.
